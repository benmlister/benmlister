Bio

Ben joined the team at Cape York Natural Resource Management in May 2015 as the IT Support Officer. He has lived in Far North Queensland his whole life and has worked in Cape York for most of his professional life.

Ben is highly experienced in multimedia and film making, knowledge and data collection, website management and web systems. Prior to joining Cape York NRM, Ben worked with Victor Steffensen at Mulong in film and sound production.
